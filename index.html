About Me

Md. Minhazur Rahman Mahim
Md. Minhazur Rahman Mahim

I'm Md. Minhazur Rahman Mahim, a third-year Biomedical Engineering student at Islamic University, Kushtia, Bangladesh (Session 2022–23). I'm passionate about the intersection of engineering, healthcare, and social impact.

Alongside my studies, I work as a campus correspondent for multiple Bengali daily newspapers and TV channels, covering university events, academic achievements, and campus issues. I believe in science communication and using journalism as a tool for positive change.

I actively participate in IEEE EMBS, Biomed Innovates, Pathakbondhu, and other campus organizations. My interests span biomedical research, youth empowerment, and environmental advocacy.
